Vassil Atanassov

Hey! I am a second year PhD (DPhil) student in Robotics with the Dynamic Robot Systems group at the Oxford Robotics Institute, University of Oxford. My main interests are in legged robot locomotion and loco-manipulation and I'm working under the supervision of Prof. Ioannis Havoutis. Previously, I graduated from the University of Glasgow with a BEng Mechanical Engineering in 2017, and from the Delft University of Technology with an MSc Robotics in 2023. My Master's thesis was on Curriculum-based DRL for agile jumping in quadruped robots under the supervision of Prof. Cosimo Della Santina.
